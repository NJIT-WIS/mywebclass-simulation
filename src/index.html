Our Story

By GetTracking Inc. Last Updated: March 22 , 2023 0

Mission

Our mission at GetTracking is to provide individuals with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to make informed decisions about their health and wellness. We are committed to delivering personalized and science-backed nutrition and exercise tracking solutions that are accessible, convenient, and easy to use. We aim to inspire and motivate our customers to take an active role in their health and wellness journey, while also fostering a sense of community and support through our platform. Our team is dedicated to continuously improving and innovating our products and services, with a focus on user experience, privacy, and security.We believe that by empowering individuals to take control of their health and wellness, we can make a positive impact on their lives and the world around us.

Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their health and wellness journey through personalized and science-backed information.
  • Transparency: We are committed to transparency and honesty in all that we do, from the information we provide to our customers to our business practices.
  • Accessibility: We believe that everyone should have access to the tools and resources they need to live a healthy and fulfilling life, which is why we strive to make our platform accessible and user-friendly for all.
  • Privacy and Security: We take the privacy and security of our customers' data very seriously, and are committed to complying with all relevant data protection regulations.
  • Innovation: We are dedicated to continuously improving and innovating our platform, with a focus on user experience, functionality, and accuracy.
  • Education: We believe in providing our customers with the knowledge and education they need to make informed decisions about their health and wellness.
  • Community: We are committed to fostering a sense of community and support among our customers, and believe that together, we can achieve more than we could alone.
  • Adaptability: We understand that everyone's needs and goals are different, which is why we strive to provide personalized recommendations and adapt to the changing needs of our customers.

Each of these values supports our mission by creating a welcoming and supportive environment for students and teachers to learn and grow. Our focus on people ensures that everyone feels included and valued, and our commitment to learning provides educational opportunities that help students and teachers succeed in the modern software industry. Our emphasis on efficiency and dependability allows us to provide a practical, reliable platform that makes the most of everyone's time.

Origin

The idea for GetTracking was born out of our own personal struggles with maintaining healthy eating habits and exercise routines as college students. We noticed that we often found ourselves grabbing fast food on the go and not paying enough attention to our nutrition needs. We knew we needed to make a change, but we also recognized that we lacked the knowledge and tools to do so effectively. As we started to research and experiment with different nutrition and exercise tracking methods, we quickly realized that there were very few resources available that provided personalized and science-backed information. We also found that many of the existing solutions were either too complex or not user-friendly enough for the average person to use. This is when we had our "aha" moment. We realized that there was a real need for a comprehensive and user-friendly nutrition and exercise tracking platform that would empower individuals to take control of their health and wellness journey. We spent months researching, developing, and refining our idea, working closely with nutrition and fitness experts to ensure that our platform would provide accurate and personalized information. We also focused on creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface that would make it easy for anyone to use, regardless of their level of tech-savviness. Today, GetTracking is a provider of personalized nutrition and exercise tracking solutions, helping thousands of individuals around the world achieve their health and fitness goals. We are proud of how far we've come and are committed to continuing to innovate and improve our platform so that we can help even more people live healthier and happier lives.

Thank you for your interest in GetTracking. Together, we can create a community of people committed to improving their nutritional habits and living healthier lifestyles.
