Our Story

By Author Name Last Updated: January 1, 2022 0

Mission

"Our mission is to establish a group of individuals dedicated to assisting students from diverse backgrounds and age groups in achieving success in the contemporary software industry through the provision of practical, experiential learning opportunities."

Values

  • People: At the heart of our community lies a profound appreciation for every individual, and we strongly believe in their potential for success, be it a student or teacher. Our unwavering commitment to fostering an all-inclusive and supportive atmosphere ensures that everyone feels welcome to learn and evolve.
  • Learning: At our core, we believe that learning is an ongoing journey, and every individual has unique knowledge and insights to share and gain. Therefore, we are dedicated to facilitating educational opportunities that enable students and teachers to develop expertise with emerging technologies and acquire in-depth knowledge of advanced software engineering concepts.
  • Efficiency: We understand that time is a precious commodity, and thus, we endeavor to optimize its usage. Our focus is on delivering a practical, experiential learning program that empowers students and teachers to acquire the essential skills required for success in the rapidly-evolving software industry promptly.
  • Dependability: We recognize the significance of dependability and consistency, and our primary objective is to deliver an unwavering and trustworthy platform for students and teachers to learn and collaborate seamlessly.

These values create a thriving community that nurtures the growth and development of every individual, be it a student or teacher. We are driven by a passion for people, which enables us to foster a welcoming and supportive environment where everyone is celebrated and encouraged to reach their full potential. By providing dynamic educational opportunities, we equip our members with the knowledge and skills to thrive in the rapidly-changing software industry. Our unwavering emphasis on efficiency and dependability further ensures that we deliver a practical and reliable platform that maximizes everyone's time, ultimately empowering them to achieve their dreams and aspirations.

Origin

Mywebclass.org was founded in 2023 by Keith Williams, a University Lecturer at New Jersey Institute of Technology with over 30 years of experience as a software engineer, entrepreneur, and 17 years of experience as a university lecturer and in k-12 education. Keith founded mywebclass.org to help bridge the gap between educators and professionals in the software industry by creating a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed.

Thank you for your interest in mywebclass.org. Together, we can create a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed in the modern software industry.
