Our Story

By: Dina Lahham | Arly Volmar | Nathan Hernandez | ChatGPT Last Updated: March 21, 2023 0

Welcome

Welcome to MyWebClass, a website dedicated to providing resources to educators who want to teach modern technology, such as cutting-ege software engineering skills, to their students. With the world becoming increasingly digital, it is important for students to have a strong foundation in these skills to prepare them for future careers. However, it can be daunting for educators who are not most up-to-date or familiar with the latest technologies available to teach these subjects effectively.

That's where we come in. Our website offers a range of resources, including lesson plans, activities, assessments, and teaching guides, all designed to make teaching technology and software engineering accessible and engaging for both educators and students. Whether you are a seasoned technology educator or new to teaching these subjects, we have something for everyone.

Mission

At MyWebClass, our mission is to empower educators with the knowledge and resources they need to teach modern technology skills, including cutting-edge software engineering, to their students. As the world becomes more digital, we believe it is critical for students to have a strong foundation in these skills to succeed in future careers. We recognize the challenges that educators may face in keeping up with the latest technologies, and we are committed to providing them with the support they need to teach these subjects effectively.

Origin

Mywebclass.org was founded in 2023 by Keith Williams, a University Lecturer at New Jersey Institute of Technology with over 30 years of experience as a software engineer, entrepreneur, and 17 years of experience as a university lecturer and in K-12 education. Keith founded mywebclass.org to help bridge the gap between educators and professionals in the software industry by creating a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed.

MyWebClass is carried forward in its latest form by Arly Volmar, Dina Lahham, and Nathan Hernandez. Each brings their unique talents and experiences in order to deliver the latest great feature enhancements and improvements necessary to continue to improve the MyWebClass customer experience!

Thank you for your interest in mywebclass.org. Together, we can create a community of people committed to helping students of all ages and backgrounds succeed in the modern software industry. Let's keep working towards this goal and make a positive impact in the lives of countless individuals!
